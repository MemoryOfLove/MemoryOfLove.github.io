无线默认的损失模型是P_r=\frac{P_tG_tG_r\lambda^2}

  • Gt: tx gain (unit-less)
  • Gr: rx gain (unit-less)
  • Pt: tx power (W)
  • d: distance (m)
  • L: system loss
  • lambda: wavelength (m)
